About Us

We are a group of Palestinian youth who created this plastic art exhibition to shed the light on the Palestinian Case in front of all the world through colours, hoping that our aspirations and suffering will reach to hearts and souls of all human beings , no matter how different cultures, languages and nationalities there are.
